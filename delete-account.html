Ethio-Tibeb — Account & Data Deletion

Help Center

Ethio-Tibeb allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the Ethio-Tibeb mobile app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select “Delete Account”.
  4. Confirm your request.

Alternatively, you can request deletion by emailing: support@ethiotibeb.com

Include the email address used to create your account.

What gets deleted

  • User profile information
  • Comments and activity
  • Favorites and watch history

What may be retained temporarily

  • Security logs for up to 30 days
  • Data required for legal compliance

After processing your request, your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.
