Ethio-Tibeb allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data. How to request account deletion: 1. Open the Ethio-Tibeb mobile app. 2. Go to Profile → Settings. 3. Select "Delete Account". 4. Confirm your request. Alternatively, you can request deletion by emailing: support@ethiotibeb.com Include the email address used to create your account. Data that will be deleted: • User profile information • Comments and activity • Favorites and watch history Data that may be retained temporarily: • Security logs for up to 30 days • Data required for legal compliance After processing your request, your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.
