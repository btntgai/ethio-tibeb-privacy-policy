By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We use this to authenticate users, sync favorites and watch history, and personalize content.

We may collect information about how you use the App:

We may collect:

This is used for security, analytics, and improving performance. We do not collect precise GPS location unless explicitly enabled.

The App displays content from third-party platforms such as YouTube and broadcasters. When you watch embedded videos:

Please review the YouTube/Google Privacy Policy.

If you enable notifications, we may send breaking news alerts, live program alerts, and content updates. You can disable notifications anytime in device settings.
